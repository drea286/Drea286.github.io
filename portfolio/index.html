  • Lens Replacement Guide
  • Sensor Replacement Guide
  • Protector Replacement Guide
    • For this instructions project, I was given a specific electronic product that had no known instructional guide, and needed one to help people keep their electronic products longer and therefore reducing e-waste. The audience for the instructions would be any English-speaker across the globe that has a Sony Alpha NEX-5N that needs replacement parts or other repairs so as to keep their camera longer, and that has access to the internet. These documents represent skills required for writing effective instructions with proper design and style choices. This series of five guides demonstrate the different style choices I chose, such as proper warning symbols or action verbs. Assessing this work shows that I understand the concise nature, active voice, and clarity needed to have successful instructions.

Funny Stuff: My Passion

By Andrea Tobar

What is this about?

Humor can have a significant positive effect on your life. It takes a good sense of humor to be able to get the best out of most situations. Humor is my life-blood and I dedicate my website to it!

Explore: my favorites!

There are so many different styles of humor out there currently, and one of my favorites: Internet humor. Jokes that take off and go viral can be strange, yet clever!
